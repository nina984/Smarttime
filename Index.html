Smart Time Management

Why many students fail to achieve great grades? Poor time management.

The Problem

Many students struggle not because they're not smart, but because they don't manage their time properly. They procrastinate, cram, or spend time on low-priority tasks.

The Solution: Eisenhower Matrix

This technique helps you prioritize tasks by urgency and importance:

Urgent & Important:
Do it now (e.g., exam revision)
Important, Not Urgent:
Plan it (e.g., long-term projects)
Urgent, Not Important:
Delegate if possible
Not Urgent & Not Important:
Avoid it (e.g., scrolling TikTok)

Meet Alex

Alex was a regular student. He used to waste time on useless tasks and cram before exams. One day, he discovered the Eisenhower Matrix. He started planning his days, focusing on important tasks first. Weeks later, his grades improved significantly. He finally had control over his time.
