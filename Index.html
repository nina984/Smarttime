The Eisenhower Matrix 🧠✅

The Eisenhower Matrix helps you sort tasks into four types:

  • Urgent & Important: Do it now!
  • Important but Not Urgent: Schedule it.
  • Urgent but Not Important: Delegate it.
  • Not Urgent & Not Important: Bye Felicia! 👋

After Discovering Time Management 📚🔥

Alex started using techniques like Pomodoro, daily planning, and Eisenhower’s Matrix. Fast forward a few weeks — BAM! — his grades started rising!
